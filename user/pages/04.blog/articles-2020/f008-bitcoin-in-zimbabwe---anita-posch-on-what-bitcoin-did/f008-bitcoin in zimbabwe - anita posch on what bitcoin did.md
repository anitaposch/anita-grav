# Bitcoin in Zimbabwe - Anita on What Bitcoin Did

>“I’m into Bitcoin because of the possibility to support human rights and support people’s lives.” — Anita

In this interview, Peter McCormack is talking with Anita, Bitcoin podcaster, speaker and author. We discuss her recent trip to Zimbabwe, free speech & freedom of expression, scams & MLM, hyperinflation and if and how Zimbabweans are using Bitcoin.
